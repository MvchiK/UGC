About

UGC is a pure PvP and eSports community bringing the biggest and baddest players together for ALL online multiplayer games. All the players need to do is sign up, post a clip, and share it with their friends! Other players will be able to directly message the poster and leave comments on their videos to send a challenge and than its up to the poster to choose their opponent.
